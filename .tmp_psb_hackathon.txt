2. Mandatory Wealth Protection Layer (Cyber-Security & Fraud)
All critical wealth actions (e.g., starting/modifying SIPs, large investments, portfolio rebalancing, surplus fund movement) must pass through a mandatory cyber-protection layer that:
 Evaluates basic risk signals such as: o Trusted vs. New device (simulated) o OTP usage patterns o Transaction amount vs. historical behavior o Speed, retries, or urgency of actions
 Generates a Wealth Protection Risk Score (Low / Medium / High)  Takes proportionate action:
o Allow action o Display warning or cooling-off message o Temporarily block or delay the action

7. Compliance Requirements

1. Customer Data Privacy & Consent · Always take clear permission from users before using their financial
data. · Explain what data you're collecting and why. · No hidden data usage!
2. Secure Handling of Financial Information · Use encryption, secure APIs, and avoid storing sensitive data in plain
text. · Ensure your prototype shows safe data flow (even if simulated).
3. Transparent & Explainable AI · Your AI/logic must not give "mysterious" investment advice. · Show how and why a recommendation was made (simple rules, or logic).
4. No Guaranteed Returns or Misleading Claims · Your system should not promise profits or claim "zero risk" investments.
