India Crypto Tax Rules (FY 2025-26)

Section 115BBH - VDA Taxation
Flat 30% tax on gains from Virtual Digital Assets. No deductions except cost of acquisition.
Tax Rate 30% (flat, regardless of income slab)
TDS Rate 1% on transfers above ₹10,000/year
Loss Set-off ❌ Not allowed (crypto losses can't offset other income)
Loss Carry Forward ❌ Not allowed
Deductions Only cost of acquisition (no expenses)
Gift Tax 30% if value > ₹50,000 from non-relative
